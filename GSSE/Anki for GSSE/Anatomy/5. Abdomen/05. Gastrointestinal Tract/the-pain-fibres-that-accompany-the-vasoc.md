### Text
<div>
  The pain fibres that accompany the vasoconstrictor (sympathetic)
  nerves give rise to {{c1::periumbilical}} pain if from midgut
  derivatives (e.g. the appendix) but to {{c1::hypogastric}} pain
  if from the hindgut.
</div>
